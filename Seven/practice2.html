i'm gonna be honest, i think rail grinders from dimensions is one of the best stages from that pack. you get to jump directly from one set of gutters to another which is a direct callback to another game in the series called banana blitz. it also has a good sense of difficulty progression as the first gutter j ump has the player rebounding off a vertical wall to help them land the jump, followed by a section containing flat spaced gaps which help prepare the player for what's to come next by increasing tension and adrenaline, which finally culminates with a second gutter jump onto a sloped set of rails absent of a vertical wall. the setting also works well thematically as the player is immersed in a dark forest setting where they must carefully watch every step they make in order to escape the forest without getting hurt or lost along the way. and, the stage is cool
